SMBs, Freelancers & Associations

You think AI and data are for big groups.

That's exactly what small internet companies thought in 2005. Those who took the step took a head start.

"It's too expensive." "My business is doing fine as it is." "It's for big structures." I hear these phrases often. And I get it — AI can seem distant, complex, reserved for those with tech teams and massive budgets.

But the reality on the ground is that data and AI can transform the daily life of a small structure as much as that of a large group — sometimes more, because the gains are immediate and visible. Save hours on repetitive tasks. Better understand your clients. Make better decisions with the data you already have. Automate what exhausts you to focus on what truly has value.

The question is not "does AI concern me?" It is "do I want to choose how it enters my organization — or endure it when it will be too late?"

My starting point is always the same: understand where you are before proposing anything. I perform a diagnostic of your data and AI maturity — not a theoretical audit, but a concrete reading of your organization, your tools, your data, and your real needs. Together, we identify where data and AI can save you time, simplify your processes, or create value — and we prioritize.

At the end of this diagnostic, you leave with a clear and actionable roadmap. You can then choose to implement certain projects with me, or with the provider of your choice. The goal is not to create dependency — it is to give you the keys.

What I offer

  • Data & AI maturity diagnostic adapted to your scale
  • Identification of concrete opportunities at your level
  • Prioritized and actionable roadmap
  • Implementation support, according to your needs and desires

For Whom?

Freelancers, startups, SMBs, associations, cooperatives, social and solidarity economy structures, independent medical or legal offices, local authorities — any structure that wants to move forward seriously on data and AI, without the complexity.

Your structure may be more ready than you think.
